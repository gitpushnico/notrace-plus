# Third-Party Notice

Last updated: 2026-04-17

This project is designed to run browser-first with no third-party analytics, no ad pixels, and no tracking cookies on first page load.

## Included Third-Party Assets

### Fonts (self-hosted)

The following fonts are distributed locally in `fonts/` and loaded from this project's own origin:

- DM Mono (Google Fonts)
- Unbounded (Google Fonts)

License files included in repository:

- `fonts/OFL-DMMono.txt`
- `fonts/OFL-Unbounded.txt`

Upstream pages:

- DM Mono: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/DM+Mono?preview.script=Latn
- DM Mono license: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/DM+Mono/license?preview.script=Latn
- Unbounded: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Unbounded
- Unbounded license: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Unbounded/license

## Runtime Third-Party Behavior

On first page load, this site is intended to avoid third-party tracking behavior:

- No third-party analytics SDKs.
- No ad/marketing pixels.
- No non-essential tracking cookies.
- No calls to Google Fonts CDN.

User-initiated actions may open external websites in a new tab (for example DuckDuckGo or linked resources). Those destinations process requests under their own policies.

## Compliance Intent

This notice documents third-party dependencies and runtime behavior for transparency, licensing, and EU privacy review.
